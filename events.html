Our Fighters

Petr Yan is a Russian professional mixed martial artist competing in the bantamweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship where he is a former UFC Bantamweight Champion. Yan formerly fought in Absolute Championship Berkut where he is a former ACB Bantamweight Champion. As of April 12, 2022 he is #1 in the UFC bantamweight rankings and #13 in the UFC men's pound-for-pound rankings. More text...

Justin Gatheji is an American professional mixed martial artist. He currently competes in the Lightweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship. He is a former Interim UFC Lightweight Champion. As of December 13, 2021, he is #1 in the UFC lightweight rankings. Read More...

Francis Ngannou is a Cameroonian mixed martial artist. He currently competes in the heavyweight division for the Ultimate Fighting Championship, where he is the current UFC Heavyweight Champion.
